About "HackLife"

HackLife is going to be a website all about the best life hacks. It will contain numerous tabs named by category
of the life hack such as "Kitchen". The entire purpose of the website is to make peoples lives a little bit better and hassle free.
I personally am pretty lazy and love finding better more efficient ways to do things, and I am hoping HackLife will help other people
by giving them good life hacks that actually work.
